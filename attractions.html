Attractions

Discover the best of Pāpāmoa

Things to do

Explore Pāpāmoa

From golden beaches to ancient hilltop pā sites — there's something for everyone.

Beach

Pāpāmoa Beach

16km of golden sand stretching along the Bay of Plenty. Lifeguard-patrolled in summer, dog-friendly year round, with free parking, showers and toilets at the reserve.

Papamoa Beach Rd Free entry

Water Sports

Hakanini Surf School

Rated 5 stars by over 300 visitors. Group and private lessons for all ages, kids' holiday programmes, and standalone board hire. Right by the surf lifesaving club.

★★★★★ Mon–Sun 8am–5pm

Nature & Culture

Pāpāmoa Hills Regional Park

Walk through native forest to panoramic views 224m above sea level. Home to 10 Māori pā sites dating back to the 1500s. Open daily 6:30am–7pm.

~45 min walk Free entry

Nature

Kaiate Falls

A stunning multi-step waterfall hidden in native bush, just 15 minutes from Pāpāmoa. Take the 1-hour loop walk through lush forest to the upper and lower falls.

15 min drive Free entry

Adventure

Blokarting

Jump in a wind-powered go-kart and tear around the track — no experience needed. Also try drift karting, a three-wheeled electric kart built for drifting. Unique to Pāpāmoa.

176 Parton Rd All ages

Markets

Little Big Markets

The best community market in the Bay — local craft stalls, live music and gourmet street food every Saturday morning at Coronation Park, Mount Maunganui.

★★★★★ Sat 9am–2pm
